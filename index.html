PlotToPlate Platform

Community-Driven Food Waste Reduction

PlotToPlate is a community-driven web platform designed to reduce food waste and foster sustainable living through connection, sharing, and collaborative gardening. Our platform targets local communities, especially around community gardens and neighborhoods, creating sustainable ecosystems where food waste is minimized.

Community Donation Forum

Share surplus food from gardens or homes with photos, quantities, and pickup locations. Connect with neighbors to prevent waste while building community bonds.

Garden Management System

Create and manage community gardens with customizable plot layouts. Claim individual plots, track resources, and coordinate with fellow gardeners.

User Profiles & Gamification

Track personal impact with real statistics, gardening streaks, and milestone achievements. Multiple user roles from Master Gardener to Garden Volunteer.

Accessible Web Platform

Built with Flask and Bootstrap for responsive design across devices. Features guest/kiosk mode for public browsing and full accessibility support.
